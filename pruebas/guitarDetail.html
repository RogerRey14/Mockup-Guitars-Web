DC-15ME

$1.999,00

With an all mahogany body, the DC-15ME produces a warm, rich, full tone. This Dreadnought acoustic-electric cutaway model is complete with an East Indian rosewood fingerboard and bridge. The beauty of the fingerboard is enhanced with diamonds and squares position inlays. New Fishman® Matrix VT Enhance™ electronics complete this model and make it a very affordable and versatile instrument for player looking for a classic, timeless model. This model is a variation on the D-15 played by Chris Martin of Coldplay.
